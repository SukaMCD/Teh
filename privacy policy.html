Privacy Policy

At Leafly Tea, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other information you voluntarily provide through contact forms, registration, or other communications.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our services, including pages visited, duration of visits, and interactions with our website or app.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve user experience and understand how you engage with our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services.
  • To send you updates, offers, or information relevant to you.
  • To improve user experience and enhance features of our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no data transmission method over the internet is 100% secure, and while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • To enforce this policy or protect our rights.
  • To comply with legal obligations or upon request from authorities.
  • To use third-party services that support our operations (such as hosting providers or analytics), who are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information that we store. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided on our site.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the bottom of this policy. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to understand how we are protecting your data.
